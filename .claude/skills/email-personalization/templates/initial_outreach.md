# Initial Outreach Email Template

## Template ID: initial_outreach_v1

### Purpose
First touch point with a prospect. Focus on establishing relevance and credibility.

### Structure

```
Subject Line: {personalized_subject}

Hi {first_name},

{opening_hook}

{relevance_statement}

{value_proposition}

{social_proof}

{call_to_action}

{signature}
```

### Guidelines

1. **Subject Line** (max 60 characters)
   - Reference company name or specific insight
   - Ask a question or state a benefit
   - Avoid spam triggers (FREE, URGENT, etc.)

2. **Opening Hook** (1-2 sentences)
   - Reference something specific about their company
   - Show you've done research
   - Never start with "I" or "My name is"

3. **Relevance Statement** (1-2 sentences)
   - Connect their situation to your solution
   - Reference a pain point or opportunity

4. **Value Proposition** (2-3 sentences)
   - State clear, specific benefits
   - Include metrics when possible
   - Focus on outcomes, not features

5. **Social Proof** (1 sentence)
   - Mention similar companies
   - Include specific results

6. **Call to Action** (1 sentence)
   - Single, clear ask
   - Make it easy to respond
   - Suggest a specific time frame

### Tone Guidelines
- Professional but conversational
- Confident but not aggressive
- Helpful, not salesy
- Specific, not generic
