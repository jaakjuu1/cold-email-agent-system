# Positive Response Reply Template

## Template ID: response_positive_v1

### Purpose
Respond quickly and professionally to a positive reply, move toward meeting.

### Structure

```
Subject Line: Re: {original_subject}

{thank_you}

{confirm_value}

{calendar_options}

{additional_context}

{signature}
```

### Guidelines

1. **Thank You** (1 sentence)
   - Express genuine appreciation
   - Match their energy level

2. **Confirm Value** (1-2 sentences)
   - Briefly reinforce what they'll get from the call
   - Build excitement

3. **Calendar Options** (1-2 sentences)
   - Offer specific times
   - Or send calendar link
   - Make it easy

4. **Additional Context** (Optional, 1 sentence)
   - What to expect on the call
   - Anything they should prepare

### Example Variables

```json
{
  "first_name": "Sarah",
  "original_subject": "Quick question about TechCorp's expansion",
  "thank_you": "Great to hear from you, Sarah! Really appreciate you taking the time.",
  "confirm_value": "I'm confident we can share some relevant insights from similar companies – especially around maintaining velocity during rapid hiring.",
  "calendar_options": "Here are a few times that work on my end:\n- Tuesday 2pm ET\n- Wednesday 10am ET\n- Thursday 3pm ET\n\nOr feel free to grab any slot here: [calendar_link]",
  "additional_context": "The call will be about 15-20 minutes – I'll come prepared with some thoughts specific to TechCorp's situation.",
  "signature": "Looking forward to it!\nJohn"
}
```

### Key Principles
- Respond within 2 hours if possible
- Make scheduling frictionless
- Don't oversell in the reply
- Keep momentum high
- Confirm meeting details clearly
