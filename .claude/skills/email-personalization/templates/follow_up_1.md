# Follow-Up Email 1 Template

## Template ID: follow_up_1_v1

### Purpose
First follow-up, typically 3-4 days after initial outreach. Add new value.

### Structure

```
Subject Line: Re: {original_subject}

Hi {first_name},

{brief_reference}

{new_value_angle}

{additional_insight}

{soft_call_to_action}

{signature}
```

### Guidelines

1. **Brief Reference** (1 sentence)
   - Don't guilt them for not responding
   - Simply acknowledge your previous email

2. **New Value Angle** (2-3 sentences)
   - Share something new and valuable
   - Industry insight, case study, or relevant content
   - NOT a repeat of the first email

3. **Additional Insight** (1-2 sentences)
   - Company-specific observation
   - Market trend relevant to them
   - Connection to their recent news

4. **Soft Call to Action** (1 sentence)
   - Lower commitment ask
   - Or same ask rephrased

### Timing
- Send 3-4 business days after initial email
- Best sent Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM recipient's timezone

### Key Principles
- Add value, don't just "bump"
- Show continued research/interest
- Keep shorter than initial email
- Maintain same thread (reply to original)
